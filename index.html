Privacy Policy for Transk
Effective Date: 23.03.25
Last Updated: 23.03.25

Introduction

Welcome to Transk, a Discord bot designed to provide seamless translation services when users react to messages with flag emojis. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.


Data Collection

Transk collects and processes the following types of data:
  • User Messages: The bot temporarily processes messages that are reacted to with supported flag emojis for the purpose of translation. Messages are not stored.
  • User IDs and Server IDs: The bot may log user and server IDs for functionality such as command execution and rate-limiting but does not permanently store personal information.
  • Data: The bot detects reactions to determine when translation services are needed but does not store reaction history.

Data Usage

The collected data is used solely to:
  • Provide real-time translation services.
  • Improve bot performance and prevent abuse.
  • Ensure proper bot functionality within Discord servers.

Data Storage & Security

  • Transk does not store user messages, translations, or reactions beyond the time required to process them.
  • Server and user IDs may be temporarily cached for performance purposes but are not retained long-term.
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect any temporarily stored data.

Third-Party Services

Transk may use third-party translation services (e.g., Google Translate, DeepL, etc.) to process translations. These services have their own privacy policies, and by using Transk, you acknowledge and agree that your messages may be processed by these third-party providers.

Your Rights & Choices

  • You can stop using Transk at any time to discontinue any data processing.
  • If you have concerns about how your data is being used, you can contact us by email.
  • Server administrators can remove the bot from their server if they no longer wish to use its services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the bot's official documentation or support server. Continued use of Transk after changes means you accept the revised policy.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email.

By using Transk, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
